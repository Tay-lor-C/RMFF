Films

Rossland Mountain Film Festival is proud to present the following adventure films for 2022

Feel Reel

Blank Collective | 60 Mins

Opening Night

Feel Real represents the past and present moments, memories and friendships that have shaped The Blank Collective into the skiers they are today.

Cuddle

Alex Honnold, Tommy Caldwell, Adam Stack | 34 mins

Opening Night

A massive, remote link-up of 17 alpine summits pushes the world’s best climbers to the brink – and into each others’ shivering arms.

Barefoot Charles

Charles Albert | 28 Mins

Dirtbag Night

An opera-singing, cave-dwelling, animal-skinning minimalist tackles futuristic boulder problems in the Fontainebleau forest.

Living on an Island

Keusti Fraun | 28 Mins

Dirtbag Night

A short film about the beauty and uniqueness of our planet and the will, fate and desire of the individual man to explore this world

Part of Me

Stephanie Dupont | 8 Mins

Slaydies Night

Stephanie Dupont's story of falling in love with mountain biking and how the other women in her local riding community inspired her to get back on the bike.

Nexus

Shannon Corsi | 43 mins

Slaydies Night

Nexus is an action sports film documenting five distinct groups of skiers, their connection to the mountains, and how skiing shapes their relationships.

Big Things to Come

Alex Johnson | 21 Mins

Slaydies Night

Elite climber Alex Johnson’s struggle with a boulder problem propels her on a decade long journey of self-discovery.

A Part of Us

Wester Sjöberg Productions | 21 Mins

Red Mountain Speaker Series

A documentary and ski movie following Jacob and Sofia Wester through the Norwegian mountains during a record winter, as they chase unskied peaks and powder snow.

Bridge Boys

Pete Whittaker, Tom Randall | 28 Mins

A horizontal big-wall adventure on the longest, hardest and most ridiculous crack climb ever attempted.

Connected to Terrain

Alberto Collantes | 12 Mins

Opening Night

We enter the world of Jonathan Trango, where we learn about the keys to proximity, a sport in which technical and psychological preparation play a very important role.

TrailBuilders

Ryan Flett | 6 Mins

For some, trail building is a job, but for most, it is a passion and for many, it is what they do in their free time with the only compensation being the satisfaction of giving back to their communities and making them better places to live.

Epic Day

Todd Larsen | 7 Mins

A Kootenay multi-sport adventure day on June 1, 2019. Hike, ski, bike, canoe, camp, climb, and a few drinks along the way. Mostly Type I fun, with a bit of Type II.

Raw Dogz

Lucas Corroto | 11 Mins

Just a typical week for Elliot Vercoe and Mark McGivern, climbing a bunch of classics, training and working at the local climbing gym Camp St.

Magic Hour

Teton Gravity Research | 60 mins

The Main Event

Magic hour is not so much a particular time a day as a culmination of powerful moments in some of the most beautiful, wild places on the planet. Between the thrill of the hunt and the peace of floating down a mountain, the sum of our experiences becoming greater than its parts is what we seek.

Voyage to the End of the World

Schirmner Film | 47 Mins

Closing Matinee

Professional skier Nikolai Schirmer barely survives his first trip on the sailboat Sofie, swearing to never set foot on her again, but is tempted back by the new captain and a legendary skier who tells him tales of the mountains of Svalbard, that island group just before the North Pole.

Free Rider

Jérôme Tanon | 28 Mins

Sam Anthamatten (ski) and Victor de Le Rue (snowboard) push new boundaries in the wildest corner of Alaska. A bush pilot drops the small crew of five on a remote, unexplored glacier to conquer the spine walls in a series of ascents, each one more committed.

Anywhere From Here

Matchstick Productions | 60 mins

ANYWHERE FROM HERE explores the world of skiing through the eyes of 12-year-old freestyle phenomenon. Through his voice, the audience hears the skiing aspirations of a kind who just wants to play and doesn’t have to ponder real life quite yet.

Chains of Habit

Zepplin Zeerip | 6.5 mins

The Main Event

When you see a beautiful picture of a person standing on top of a big mountain, it's easy to assume their life, one of adventure, must be bliss. It's simple, right? No screens, no deadlines, just the awe of nature and moving your body in places most of us only dream of.

Beyond Begbie

Zoya Lynch | 15 mins

Red Mountain Speaker Series

Standing tall over the Revelstoke valley Mount Begbie has inspired an entire community. It is iconicized in the logos and names of local businesses, featured heavily in artists’ works, is photographed by every visiting tourist, and is explored by the outdoor enthusiast - no matter the season.

Fly Monarca

Benjamin Jordan | 76 mins

Closing Matinee

In pursuit of a dream, and inspired by a butterfly, an ambitious paraglider pilot attempts to fly across the United States, from Mexico to Canada, relying solely on the sun, wind and his own willpower.

Peroots

Andreu Lacondeguy | 15 Mins

It's been a few years since we planned a trip to South America. I wanted to take my bike to a different place, get out of the routine and explore, since I think that's the definition of our sport.

Hot Cocoa

Maria Thomsen | 30 mins
